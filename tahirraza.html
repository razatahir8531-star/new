Application Collection

1. Application to be the Chief Guest on Sports Day

Subject: Application to be the Chief Guest on Sports Day Respected Sir / Madam, With due respect, I beg to state that I am a student of your esteemed college. Our annual Sports Day will be held soon, and we would be greatly honoured if you could be the Chief Guest. Your presence would encourage the students and make the event memorable for everyone. I shall be extremely obliged if you kindly consider my request and take necessary action in this regard.

2. Application for Sick Leave

Subject: Application for Sick Leave Respected Sir / Madam, With due respect, I beg to state that I have been suffering from high fever and cough for the last two days. The doctor has advised me to take complete rest at home. Therefore, I am unable to attend college during this period. Kindly grant me leave.

3. Character Certificate

I have completed my studies in your college and now I need a character certificate. It is required for my higher studies and future employment. Kindly issue the certificate at your earliest convenience.

4. Remission of Absence Fine

I was unable to attend classes due to a family emergency. For this reason, I have been fined for absence. I request you to kindly remit my fine considering my genuine reason.

5. Full Fee Concession

I belong to a poor family and it is difficult for me to pay my full college fee. My father’s income is very low, and I depend entirely on him. I request you kindly to grant me a full fee concession.

6. Remission of Library Fine

I could not return a library book on time because I was unwell. I have now returned the book with a fine notice. Kindly remit the library fine as my delay was not intentional.

Short Application Requests

7. Refund of Security: I have cleared all my dues and returned books/hostel items. Kindly arrange to refund my security deposit.
8. Financial Help: My family is facing financial problems. I am a sincere student. Kindly grant me financial help from the special fund.
9. Re-admission: I could not continue studies last session due to personal problems. Now I wish to rejoin. Kindly allow re-admission.
10. College Leaving Certificate: I have completed my studies. I need a leaving certificate for admission elsewhere. Kindly issue it.
11. Change of Subject: I wish to change from Economics to Political Science as the present subject is difficult. Kindly allow this change.
12. Apology for Misbehaviour: I am deeply sorry for my misbehaviour. It was unintentional. Kindly forgive me and allow me to continue classes.
13. Scholarship: I obtained good marks but belong to a poor family. Kindly grant me a scholarship to continue my studies.
14. Study Tour: Our class is planning a study tour for practical knowledge. Kindly grant permission and necessary funds.
15. One-Month Leave: I have to go to my native place for family work. Kindly grant me one month’s leave.
16. Condoning Shortage of Lectures: My attendance fell short due to illness. Kindly condone the shortage and allow me to sit for exams.
17. Medicines in Dispensary: The dispensary lacks medicines. Kindly arrange for necessary medicines for students.
18. Leave for Urgent Work: I have urgent personal work out of station. Kindly grant me leave for one day.
19. Leave to Attend Sick Mother: My mother is seriously ill. I need to care for her. Kindly grant me leave for a few days.
20. Leave to Attend Funeral: A close relative passed away. I must attend the funeral. Kindly grant me leave for the day.

Story Introduction / کہانی کا تعارف

English Version

Stories have always been a mirror of life, reflecting human emotions, experiences, and lessons. Every story carries a message and takes us on a journey through imagination. The following story is an attempt to weave words into meaningful expressions that entertain, inspire, and teach valuable lessons about life.

"Every story teaches us something valuable. This story is about life. Let us read and learn from it."

اردو ترجمہ

کہانی کا تعارف: کہانیاں ہمیشہ سے زندگی کا عکس رہی ہیں، جو انسانی جذبات، تجربات اور سبق کی عکاسی کرتی ہیں۔ ہر کہانی ایک پیغام لے کر آتی ہے اور ہمیں تخیل کی دنیا میں سفر پر لے جاتی ہے۔ درج ذیل کہانی الفاظ کو معنی خیز انداز میں پرونے کی ایک کوشش ہے جو تفریح فراہم کرے، حوصلہ بڑھائے اور زندگی کے قیمتی اسباق سکھائے۔

"یہ کہانی انسانی فطرت اور زندگی کے تجربات کو بیان کرتی ہے اور تفریح کے ساتھ اہم سبق بھی پیش کرتی ہے۔"
